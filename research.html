Research Interests for Doctoral Studies

Edge AI & TinyML

Developing lightweight machine learning models that can run on resource-constrained IoT devices without cloud dependency. Exploring federated learning for distributed intelligence.

  • Model compression techniques
  • Federated learning protocols
  • Hardware-aware neural architecture search

Computer Vision for Accessibility

Creating vision-based assistive technologies for people with disabilities. Focus on real-time processing, low-power implementations, and multimodal interaction.

  • Real-time object detection for navigation
  • Scene understanding for accessibility
  • Multimodal sensor fusion

Sustainable AI & IoT

Designing energy-efficient AI systems and IoT networks that minimize environmental impact while maximizing performance.

  • AI-driven energy optimization
  • Green computing for IoT
  • Sustainable smart cities

Healthcare AI

Applying AI and IoT to healthcare challenges, particularly in remote monitoring, early diagnosis, and assistive technologies.

  • Wearable health monitoring
  • AI for early disease detection
  • Telemedicine platforms
