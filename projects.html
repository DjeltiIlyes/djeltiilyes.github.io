LUNAV

Intelligent Navigation System for the Blind and Visually Impaired

AI Computer Vision IoT AR/VR Python

Problem Statement

Over 285 million people worldwide are visually impaired, facing daily challenges with independent navigation and obstacle avoidance.

Solution

Developed a wearable AI-powered device that combines real-time computer vision, spatial audio feedback, and haptic vibrations to create a comprehensive navigation aid.

Technical Implementation

  • Computer Vision: YOLO object detection with custom-trained model on obstacle dataset
  • Audio Navigation: 3D spatial audio system for directional guidance
  • Hardware: Raspberry Pi 4, Ultrasonic sensors, Bone conduction headphones
  • Software Stack: Python, OpenCV, TensorFlow, Unity for AR visualization

Results

  • 95% accuracy in obstacle detection within 5 meters
  • Real-time processing at 30 FPS
  • Battery life: 8+ hours of continuous use
  • Received highest academic score: 19.5/20

DesertCool

Sustainable Cooling Solution for Arid Regions

IoT Sustainability Sensor Networks Arduino

Challenge

Temperatures in the Algerian Sahara can reach 50°C+, creating extreme living conditions and high energy consumption for cooling.

Innovation

Designed a passive cooling system using evaporative techniques enhanced with IoT monitoring and smart control.

System Design

  • Core Technology: Two-stage evaporative cooling with ceramic cooling pads
  • IoT Monitoring: Temperature, humidity, and air quality sensors
  • Smart Control: Automated fan speed and water flow adjustment
  • Energy Source: Solar-powered with battery backup

Achievements

  • 2nd Place in "The Call of Tech" National Competition 2023
  • Temperature reduction: 10-15°C in test environments
  • Energy savings: 80% compared to conventional AC
  • Water efficiency: 40% less than traditional evaporative coolers
